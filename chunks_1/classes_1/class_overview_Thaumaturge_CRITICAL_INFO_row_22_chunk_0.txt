Title: Thaumaturge

Overview: Thaumaturge specialize in battering enemies with long combos, while they heal up their allies. The [[Blade (XC3)|Blade]] of the Thaumaturge class is the Martial Linkstaff. Thaumaturge is the class of [[Teach]]. 

It is unlocked by completing the [[Hero Quest]] ''[[Going Beyond Power]]''. The [[Class Rank (XC3)|Class Rank]] maximum can be increased to 20 by completing the Ascension Quest ''[[Shadow of Enmity]]''.
