Title: Stalker

Overview:  
Stalkers wield a bow and specialize at sniping [[Enemy (XC3)|enemies]] with arrows and hunting them from the safety of stealth; while it has low attack power compared to other Attacker classes, it is better able to manage its aggro levels. It is the class of [[Juniper]].

It is unlocked by completing ''[[Natural Selection]]''. The [[Class Rank (XC3)|Class Rank]] maximum is increased to 20 by completing the [[Hero Quest|Ascension Quest]] ''[[Survivors]]''.
