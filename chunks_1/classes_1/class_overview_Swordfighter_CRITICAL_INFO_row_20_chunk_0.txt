Title: Swordfighter

Overview:  
Swordfighters specialize in close-range attacks and they have balanced abilities, especially strong at fighting powerful [[Boss (XC3)|bosses]] and [[Unique Monster (XC3)|Unique Monsters]]. The [[Blade (XC3)|Blade]] of the Swordfighter class is the Veiled Sword.  Swordfighter is the initial class of [[Noah]].
