Title: Seraph

Overview:  
Seraph is the class of [[Cammuravi]]. It employs the use of self-damaging Arts to greatly increase one's damage at lower health values.
It is unlocked after completing the [[Hero Quest]] ''[[A Twist of Fate]]''. The [[Class Rank (XC3)|Class Rank]] maximum is increased to 20 by reaching rank 10 in both Seraph and [[Flash Fencer]] classes, and going to the now-unlocked lab in Colony Omega.
