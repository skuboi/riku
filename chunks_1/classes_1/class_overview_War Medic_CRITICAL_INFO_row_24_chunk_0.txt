Title: War Medic

Overview: War Medics specialize in combat support, such as recovery and strengthening the abilities of their companions. They have a [[Battle Arts (XC3)|Talent Art]] that can [[Hit Points (XC3)|heal]] all of their allies. War Medic is the class of [[Valdi]].

It is unlocked by completing the [[Hero Quest]] ''[[The Kind Right Hand]]''. The [[Class Rank (XC3)|Class Rank]] limit can be increased to 20 by completing the Ascension Quest ''[[I'm a Mechanic]]''.
