Title: Tactician

Overview:  
Tacticians specialize in supporting allies and disrupting [[Enemy (XC3)|enemies]] by using ether to disguise them. While putting enemies to sleep, he also reduces damage with [[Battle Arts (XC3)|arts]] making evasion easier for his companions. The [[Battle Arts (XC3)|Talent Art]] of Tactician is [[Tidal Wave]]. It is the initial class of [[Taion]].
