Title: Royal Summoner

Overview:  
Royal Summoner is the class of [[Melia]]. The [[Blade (XC3)|Blade]] of the Royal Summoner class is the Elemental Staff, which is Melia's [[World Ender]] from {{XCFC}}.

It is unlocked by going to the [[Main Castle Gate]] in [[Keves Castle]] after completing the main story, or in [[New Game + (XC3)|New Game +]]. Completing the [[Hero Quest|Ascension Quest]] ''[[From Atop Her Throne]]'' will increase this class's rank limit from 10 to 20.
