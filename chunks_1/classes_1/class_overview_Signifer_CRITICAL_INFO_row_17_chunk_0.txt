Title: Signifer

Overview:  
Signifers specialize in giving enhancement or support effects to allies. It is the class of [[Fiona]].

It is unlocked by completing the [[Hero Quest]] ''[[Transparent Dreams]]''. The [[Class Rank (XC3)|Class Rank]] maximum can be increased to 20 by completing the Ascension Quest ''[[A Farewell Reset]]''.
